TUNEage

This full stack web app (complete with front and backend, as well as a Django database), was my attempt at an all-in-one app solution for modern musicians on the go. Although all features are still under development, the app features: A user-generated artist database; a lyrics archive similar to Genius; a browser compatible 'digital audio workstation', with fully-functioning 'drum rack' and 'piano roll', as well as a native media player that overlays lyrics onscreen!
